career Description

Mid-Level Software Engineer

Do you have extensive experience with a variety of programming languages and frameworks? Can you be given a general set of requirements, find your way around a new codebase, and go off to the races with minimal supervision? Do you love working remotely and being well-compensated for your time? If these are true, we'd love to talk with you.

We serve a variety of customers, primarily DoD in the SATCOM industry. Our primary programming languages are C#, Java, and Angular.

Requirements:
  • Must be a US Citizen as our client works with the US Government
  • Must be able to pass a drug test and background check
  • Preferred to have 3+ years recent experience with Angular 2+ and TypeScript and 3-6 years of overall development experience
  • Preferred to have 2+ years experience with .NET core
  • Preferred to have 2+ years experience with Java
  • The ideal candidate will have built web applications with .NET core on the back end and Angular on the front end
  • Experience with SATCOM is a plus
  • An Active DoD Clearance is not required but is a huge plus especially for follow-on work. We can arrange holding your clearance.
